# Introduction

The generations covered in the theory are:

- Greatest Generation: 最伟大的一代 (1922 - 1927)
- Silent Generation: 沉默的一代 (1928 - 1945)
- Baby Boomers: 婴儿潮一代 (1946 - 1964)
- Generation X: X 世代 (1965 - 1980)
- Milennial Generation: 千禧一代/Y 世代 (1981 - 1996)
- Generation Z: Z 世代 (1997 - 2012)
- Generation Alpha: 阿尔法世代 (2013 - 2022)
